About NutriFy

NutriFy is a cutting-edge AI-powered nutrition analysis platform designed specifically for Indian cuisine. Built with state-of-the-art deep learning models, it consists of YOLOv8n-seg an Instance segmentation model to deliver accurate, instant nutritional insights.

Our mission is to make healthy eating easier by providing instant, accurate nutritional information for Indian food, helping users make informed dietary choices and maintain a balanced lifestyle.
